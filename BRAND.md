# Adaptive Security — Brand Context

**Company:** Adaptive Security
Enterprise AI-powered security awareness training and phishing simulation platform.

## Brand Concept: "The Lens"
Adaptive is the medium through which deception becomes visible. Things that were invisible become clear.

## Visual System: Line Displacement
Structured parallel lines expand and contract to reveal imagery, headlines, icons, and forms hidden within them. The image already exists in the grid; displacement makes it readable. This isn't decoration — it's a mechanic tied to the brand concept. Supporting techniques: barrier grid, moiré effects.
